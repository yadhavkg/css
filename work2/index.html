MALALA YOUSAFZAI

Malala Yousafzai was born on July 12, 1997, in Mingora, Swat Valley, Pakistan. She was given her first name Malala, which means “grief-stricken,” after Malalai of Maiwand, a famous Pashtun poet and warrior woman from southern Afghanistan. Malala’s family is Sunni Muslim and belongs to the Yusufzai tribe.
